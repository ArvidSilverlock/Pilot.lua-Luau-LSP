Engine

It acts similar to a motor, but is powered by gasoline instead of electricity.

It is a craftable and spawnable non-flammable solid.

It cannot be resized.

At its default size (2x2x4) it has a durability of 13.

By default, its colour is #635f62.

It requires 2 Iron and 2 Pipe to be crafted.

Configurables

EngineSpeed

Determines the speed at which the engine is driven. It is a number. It ranges between -20 and 20.

Events

Configured(configurerId)

Fires when the object is configured.

The configurerId parameter is the UserId of the player who configured the object. It is a number.
